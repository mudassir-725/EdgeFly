Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search a flight? expand_more

Searching a flight on EdgeFly is simple. Use the search bar on the homepage to enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and number of passengers. Click "Search Flights" and our system, powered by Amadeus, will show you the best available options. You can then filter results and select your preferred flight to proceed with Journey.

How does the flight search engine work? expand_more

Our flight search engine quickly scans available flights across multiple airlines and travel providers. Simply enter your departure and destination cities, travel dates, and number of passengers, and our system will display a list of available flights. You can filter and sort the results based on your preferences, like price, flight duration, or departure times.

Does the flight search engine show all available airlines? expand_more

Yes, Our flight search engine shows flights from a wide range of airlines and travel agencies. However, some low-cost carriers or regional airlines may not always appear. If youâ€™re looking for a specific airline, we recommend using the filters to narrow down your search to that airline.

How accurate are the flight prices? expand_more

Flight prices displayed are generally accurate at the time of the search. However, prices can fluctuate due to changes in airline availability, demand, or other factors. If the price changes before you make your booking, we will notify you during the booking process.
